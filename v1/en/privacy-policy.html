Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

At My Baby: Care & Tracker, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share your information when you use our application or website.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you provide directly to us, such as your name, email address, and details about your baby (such as date of birth or name) to personalize your experience.

2. Use of Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our services.
  • Personalize content and baby care advice.
  • Send important communications and updates.
  • Improve the functionality of our platform.

3. What data do we store?

To provide a personalized experience, we store the following data:

  • Account information: Name and email address.
  • Baby's information: Name, date and place of birth, weight and height at birth, and sex (if provided).
  • Lists: Task lists (if provided).
  • Photographs: Photographs of the baby and parents (if provided).
  • Baby's medical data: Baby's medical records (if provided).
  • Calendar events: Calendar events related to the baby (if provided).
  • Preferences: Configuration settings within the application.

4. Retention period

We retain your personal data only as long as your account is active or as necessary to provide you with the application's services.

If the account remains inactive for a period longer than 24 months, we reserve the right to automatically delete the associated data, unless otherwise required by law.

5. How to request the deletion of your data

If you wish to permanently delete your account and all associated data, you can do so in the following ways:

A. From the application

Go to the Profile or Settings section and look for the "Delete Account" option. Follow the on-screen instructions to confirm the action.

B. By email

Send a request from the email address associated with your account to:

wolfappsdevelopment@gmail.com

Please include in the subject line: "Data Deletion Request". We will process your request within a maximum period of 30 business days.

6. Consequences of deletion

Please note that once the deletion is processed:

  • All your personal information and the baby's data will be permanently erased from our servers.
  • You will not be able to regain access to your account or previously saved records.

7. Data Protection

We implement technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, loss, or alteration.

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze site traffic. You can set your browser to refuse all cookies, although this may affect the service's functionality.

9. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services (such as analytics tools) that may collect information sent by your browser as part of a web page request.

10. Your Rights

You have the right to access, rectify, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, you can contact us through the means provided below.
