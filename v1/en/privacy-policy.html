Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

At Baby Tracker: Breastfeeding, we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information, including data related to premium subscriptions and usage analytics.

1. Information We Collect

  • User and Baby Data: Name, email, date of birth, gender, and health/activity logs.
  • Subscription Data: Subscription status, purchase history, and an anonymous user ID to manage premium access.
  • Technical Data: Device identifiers, hardware model, and operating system version.

2. Subscriptions and Payments

The app offers subscription plans (monthly, yearly, or as available) for Premium features. To manage this, we use external providers:

Google Play Console (Google LLC): All payments are processed through the Google Play platform. We do not store or have access to your credit card data or sensitive banking information.

RevenueCat (RevenueCat, Inc.): We use this tool to manage and validate your subscription status. RevenueCat receives an anonymous identifier and purchase receipt details to ensure premium features are active on your device. View their policy at revenuecat.com/privacy.

3. Data Analytics (Firebase Analytics)

We use Firebase Analytics (provided by Google) to understand how users interact with the app. This data is collected in an aggregated and pseudonymous way.

  • Purpose: To measure app performance, identify crashes, and analyze which features are most used.
  • Data Collected: Number of users and sessions, session duration, device models, and approximate geographic location (country/city).

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as your account is active or as necessary to provide services and manage active subscriptions. If the account remains inactive for more than 24 months, we reserve the right to delete the associated data automatically.

5. Data Deletion

You can permanently delete your account and associated data through the Settings menu in the app or by emailing wolfappsdevelopment@gmail.com.

Note: Deleting your account does not automatically cancel your Google Play subscription; this must be done in the Play Store settings.

6. Contact

For questions about your privacy or subscriptions: wolfappsdevelopment@gmail.com
