Data Deletion Instructions

At Mi Primer Bebé, we respect your right to privacy and to control your personal data. Below, we detail what information we store and how you can request its permanent deletion.

1. What data do we store?

To provide a personalized experience, we store the following data:

  • Account information: Name and email address.
  • Baby's information: Name, date and place of birth, weight and height at birth, and sex (if provided).
  • Lists: Task lists (if provided).
  • Photographs: Photographs of the baby and parents (if provided).
  • Baby's medical data: Baby's medical records (if provided).
  • Calendar events: Calendar events related to the baby (if provided).
  • Preferences: Configuration settings within the application.

2. Retention period

We retain your personal data only as long as your account is active or as necessary to provide you with the application's services.

If the account remains inactive for a period longer than 24 months, we reserve the right to automatically delete the associated data, unless otherwise required by law.

3. How to request the deletion of your data

If you wish to permanently delete your account and all associated data, you can do so in the following ways:

A. From the application

Go to the Profile or Settings section and look for the "Delete Account" option. Follow the on-screen instructions to confirm the action.

B. By email

Send a request from the email address associated with your account to:

wolfappsdevelopment@gmail.com

Please include in the subject line: "Data Deletion Request". We will process your request within a maximum period of 30 business days.

4. Consequences of deletion

Please note that once the deletion is processed:

  • All your personal information and the baby's data will be permanently erased from our servers.
  • You will not be able to regain access to your account or previously saved records.
