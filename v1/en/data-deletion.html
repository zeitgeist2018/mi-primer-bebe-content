Data Deletion Instructions

At My Baby: Care & Tracker, we guarantee your right to be forgotten and the removal of your personal information in accordance with data protection regulations.

1. What data is permanently deleted?

Upon requesting deletion, we will remove the following from our active servers (Firebase):

  • Your user profile (name and email address).
  • All baby records: breastfeeding, sleep, weight, height, and medical data.
  • Photographs, to-do lists, and synchronized calendar events.
  • Personalized settings and preferences.
Note on Premium Subscriptions: Deleting your account within the app DOES NOT automatically cancel your active subscription on Google Play. Since payments are managed by Google, you must cancel your subscription through the Play Store to avoid future charges.

2. Data managed by third parties

For service management purposes, certain technical data is handled as follows after deletion:

  • RevenueCat: Transaction history is kept in an anonymized form for legal and accounting compliance but is decoupled from your personal identity.
  • Firebase Analytics: Previous usage data remains in aggregated statistical reports that do not allow for individual user identification.

3. Deletion Procedure

A. Automatic Deletion (Recommended)

Within the app, go to Settings > Delete Account. This process is immediate and irreversible.

B. Manual Request

If you do not have access to the app, send an email from the address associated with your account to:

wolfappsdevelopment@gmail.com

Subject: "Data Deletion Request". We will process the manual removal from all associated services within a maximum period of 30 days.

4. Retention due to Inactivity

If the account shows no activity for a period of 24 months, the system may proceed with the automatic deletion of data to ensure the security of old information.
